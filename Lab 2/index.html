Welcome to a Touhou Fansite!

This website was made for CSE108

Touhou Project is a doujin game series created by Jun'ya Ota (AKA ZUN)

It's something that everyone can enjoy with media in manga, games, and anime! All made by the community.
Since 1997, August 15th, Touhou has been created by this man, often working only by himself or a few confidants.
Touhou has a wonderful community because of their copyright laws, allowing people to use almost any assets like music or images in their own fanworks.
